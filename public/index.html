Varia Redirect

Seamlessly redirect browser downloads to your local Varia (Aria2) instance

Take Control of Your Downloads

Varia Redirect is a powerful browser extension that automatically redirects your downloads to a local Varia (Aria2) instance, giving you advanced download management, filtering capabilities, and better control over your download queue.

Quick Setup

1

Download the Extension

Choose the version for your browser (Chrome or Firefox) and download the extension package.

2

Install the Extension

Extract the zip file and load the extension in developer mode, or install it directly if available from the browser store.

3

Configure Aria2 Connection

Open the extension options and configure your local Aria2 RPC connection settings (usually http://localhost:6800/jsonrpc).

4

Start Downloading

That's it! Your downloads will now be automatically redirected to Aria2 based on your configured filters.
